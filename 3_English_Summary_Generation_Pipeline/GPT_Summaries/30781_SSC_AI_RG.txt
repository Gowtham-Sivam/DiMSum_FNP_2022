Corero Network Security reported strong performance of its flagship SmartWall product, with customer wins across target markets such as service providers, cloud providers, and digital enterprises. The company saw a 320% increase in existing customer add-on orders compared to the previous year, and a high customer satisfaction rate with over 95% renewal rates for support and services. Corero also secured its first two $1.0 million customers and experienced an encouraging uptake of its DDoS protection as-a-service, which was launched in late 2016. The company's revenue reached $8.5 million, with a reduced EBITDA loss of $5.1 million, compared to $6.4 million in 2016. Recurring revenue growth was up 125% over the prior year, and the company had a net cash of $1.4 million at the end of 2017. Corero also signed partnerships with global blue-chip technology companies such as Juniper Networks, McAfee, and Gigamon to accelerate its revenue growth strategy, and launched new technology after a two-year development program, including a 100Gbps capacity product and a vNTD virtual software appliance.Corero is focused on enhancing the security and availability of the Internet by deploying innovative DDoS mitigation solutions. With DDoS attacks increasing in size, frequency, and complexity, service providers, cloud providers, and Internet-connected businesses require real-time protection against this evolving threat landscape. Corero's SmartWall family of products can be deployed in various topologies and utilizes modern DDoS mitigation architecture to automatically and surgically remove DDoS attack traffic while allowing good user traffic to flow uninterrupted. The company's key operational centers are in Marlborough, Massachusetts, USA, and Edinburgh, UK. Corero's real-time DDoS mitigation solution aims to protect customer service availability, revenues, and brand reputations from harmful DDoS attacks. The solutions offer high performance, automated DDoS protection at unprecedented scale with the lowest total cost of ownership for customers. These solutions are designed to provide real-time attack mitigation with continuous threat visibility, enabling the monetization of DDoS protection as-a-service offering for service providers. The Corero SmartWall protects against the latest breed of DDoS attacks in seconds, including volumetric and multi-vector attacks.Corero Network Security is well positioned to become the leading player in the real-time DDoS mitigation market with its SmartWall solution, which has been validated by over 90 customers. In 2017, the company made significant progress in growing its customer base, adding high-profile clients such as its first US federal government customer, two one million dollar customers, and its first Australian customer. Corero also improved its SmartWall DDoS defense technology, adding new attack defense and enhanced forensics and analytics capabilities. The company further expanded its product portfolio with the introduction of a 100Gbps capable product and a virtual software appliance for hybrid on-premises/cloud DDoS mitigation deployments. Corero's target market focus has been validated by customer wins in service providers, cloud providers, and digital enterprises. The company has also expanded its routes to market by signing agreements with major global technology companies such as Juniper Networks, Gigamon, and McAfee. Corero's DDoS protection as-a-service model, launched in late 2016, gained traction in 2017 with 16 customers by year-end. The company is confident in its ability to deliver revenue growth in 2018, driven by strong market demand, pending regulations, go-to-market partner recruitment, and investment in channel sales development.
