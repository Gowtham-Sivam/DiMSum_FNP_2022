Arena Group Chairman Ken Hanna presented the company's first annual results as a publicly quoted company, highlighting its successful first year on the AIM market since listing on 25 July 2017. The Board, currently composed of four directors, has adopted high governance standards while maintaining flexibility for the executive team to manage the business. The company achieved excellent financial results, delivered several major contracts, and created a strong leadership team in 2017. The Board recommended a final dividend payment of 0.9 pence for the year ending 31 December 2017, giving a total dividend of 1.35 pence per share, subject to approval at the Annual General Meeting on 24 May 2018. The final dividend will be paid in July 2018 to shareholders on the Register of Members at the close of business on 8 June 2018. The Group will continue to adopt the highest operational standards and deliver the “Arena Standard” throughout its operations, while exploring value accretive acquisition opportunities in line with its stated strategy. Hanna expressed his gratitude to all Arena employees for their hard work and dedication, which differentiates the company from its competitors in a fragmented and competitive industry.Arena UK & Europe moved into a new six-acre state-of-the-art site in April 2017, providing a centralized operations base for structures, seating, interior design, carpentry, and ice rinks. The new St Ives hub was supported by the creation of a new sales and project management office in Membury and a central furniture warehouse. The acquisition of GLD Productions added experienced staff, new product ranges, and new clients to the furniture hire division. In 2018, the company renewed many long-term contracts and secured new projects, including the Ryder Cup in Paris and opportunities related to the Rugby World Cup and Olympics in Tokyo. Arena Americas posted solid results in 2017, launching Well Dressed Tables in Milwaukee and Illinois markets. The division has set aggressive growth targets for the next five years. Key events included the US PGA Championship and Winter Village at Bryant Park. In the Middle East and Asia, Arena introduced new products such as the Arena Super Deck and Arena Seating. The division continues to grow, with a presence in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Riyadh, Kuala Lumpur, Singapore, Seoul, and Hong Kong. Upcoming events include the Rugby World Cup and Olympic Games in Japan.
