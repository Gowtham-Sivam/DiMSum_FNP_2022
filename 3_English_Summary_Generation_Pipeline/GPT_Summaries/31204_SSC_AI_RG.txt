Hilton Food Group's 2017 financial performance saw strong operating profits, with revenue growth of 10.1% and EBITDA of £56.7m. The company expanded its global footprint with the acquisition of Seachill, a leading chilled UK-based fish processor, for £81m, and signed a joint venture agreement with Sonae Modelo Continente, Portugal's leading food retailer. Hilton also announced plans to construct a new meat processing facility in Auckland, New Zealand, and expand its operations in Central Europe. The company's net cash position at the end of 2017 was £25.4m, and the Board recommended a final dividend of 14.0p per ordinary share, an increase of 11.1% compared to the previous year. Hilton's focus on strategic objectives, business development, and risk management has positioned the company for further growth in the coming years.
