The financial highlights of Paragon Banking Group PLC's 2017 Annual Report and Accounts show an underlying profit before tax of £145.2 million, a 1.0% increase from 2016. The dividend per share increased by 16.3% to 15.7 pence, and basic earnings per share rose by 6.4% to 43.1 pence. The capital CET 1 ratio remained strong at 15.9%. Profit before tax was £144.8 million, a 1.1% increase from the previous year. The return on tangible equity increased to 13.4% from 12.9% in 2016.
