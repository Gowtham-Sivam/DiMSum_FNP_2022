Automotive business grew 14%, benefiting from 2016 acquisitions and investments in Emerging Markets.In the Chief Executive's review, S.C. Harris highlights the strong performance of Bodycote in 2017, with revenue growth and cost discipline contributing to a 24% increase in headline operating profit. The return on sales also improved, rising from 16.6% to 18.0%. The full-year ordinary dividend was 17.4p, up 10%, and a special dividend of 25.0p was declared.
